This browser is managed by the PIE Lab at University of California, Irvine. But it takes a community effort to keep the browser up-to-date, and we welcome contributions from others. The list is used by researchers around the world, so adding your work is a great way of promoting it to other scholars.

We require all indexed publications be peer reviewed, in English, archival, and relevant to personal informatics. Typical publication venues include (but are not limited to) CHI, IMWUT, PervasiveHealth, and CSCW.
